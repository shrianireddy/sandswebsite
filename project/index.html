About S AND S Automation

S AND S Automation is a trusted leader in industrial automation, electrical, electronics, and instrumentation solutions in India. Established in 2019, we are headquartered in Secunderabad, Telangana, with an additional office in Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, serving clients across cement, steel, power, oil & gas, textile, pharmaceutical, and process industries.

Our mission is to help industries enhance efficiency, safety, and quality control through innovative automation and control systems. We deliver customized solutions that integrate seamlessly with existing operations, enabling our clients to optimize performance, reduce operational costs, and safeguard their workforce and equipment.

Our offerings include precision pressure control systems, flow measurement devices, furnace and boiler monitoring systems, PLC automation panels, pneumatics, industrial valves and pumps, cable management systems, junction boxes, and specialized fabrication services. Every solution we provide is backed by technical expertise, reliable service, and a dedication to customer satisfaction.

With a strong customer base in Andhra Pradesh, Telangana, and across India, S AND S Automation continues to set industry benchmarks for innovation, integrity, and timely delivery. Whether you need automation integration, industrial instrumentation supply, or complete plant control solutions, we are your go-to partner.
